    About Me

  • I am a Computer Engineering graduate from Shiraz, Iran, where I was born and raised. I have always been passionate about art, fantasy, and storytelling. These interests have led me to explore world-building, where I can bring my ideas and creative visions to life. Game development became the perfect way for me to share these imagined worlds with others, allowing people to experience and enjoy the stories I create. With a strong foundation in computer engineering, I focus on software and game development while also pursuing my love for digital art and design.

Eglantine is a Metroidvania action-adventure game that combines exploration, character development, and choice-driven storytelling. Players navigate a vast interconnected world, making decisions that shape the narrative and influence character growth, all while engaging in platforming and combat.
